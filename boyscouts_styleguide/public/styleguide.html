Typography

According to the Boy Scouts of America Brand Guidelines, Arial should be used for all website materials. It is a clean, easy-to-read web font, that is also extremely ubiquitous, making it much more likely that the majority of users will be able to see the site as intended, and get the same experience regardless of device. In order to add some variety throughout the page, Arial and Arial Narrow were used, along with three different colors, capitilzation, italics, block background colors, and various weights.
