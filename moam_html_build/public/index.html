On A Mission

Jessica Brown has answered a calling to serve, packing up and moving from Minnesota to Baja California, Mexico to work as a missionary. For a year, she will be living at the Vicente Guerro Mission, working in the clinic and striving to serve and minister to those who are in need. This site will be a place for her to share stories about daily life at the Mission, and testify to the experiences and work that she is doing and to share her journey in faith.
